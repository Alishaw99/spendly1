Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Data We Collect

We collect the information you provide when you register (name, email address, password) and the expense records you create (amount, category, date, description). We do not collect payment information.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to operate Spendly — to display your expenses, generate summaries, and maintain your account. We do not use your financial data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond providing the service.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text. You may delete your account at any time, which permanently removes all associated data from our systems.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly uses Google Fonts to render typefaces on this page. No personal or financial data is shared with third parties. We do not integrate with any analytics, advertising, or data broker services.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out at privacy@spendly.app. We aim to respond within 5 business days.
